Meet Our Team

Clinical Supervisor

ALIA AMER, M.Sc.Psych, M.ADS, BCBA

I am a Board-Certified Behavior Analyst with extensive experience working with children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) and various developmental needs. My expertise spans special education classrooms, small groups, and one-on-one settings. I excel in conducting assessments and designing interventions rooted in the principles of applied behavior analysis. My advanced knowledge of ASD allows me to understand its impact on behavior development, daily living, communication, and social skills. I am skilled in creating individualized plans that cater to each child's unique requirements. Moreover, I am proficient in providing training to professionals, parents, caregivers, and community mediators. With a deep understanding of the characteristics of ASD, I strive to make a positive difference in the lives of children by implementing effective strategies and interventions. I am passionate about helping individuals with developmental needs reach their fullest potential and lead fulfilling lives.
